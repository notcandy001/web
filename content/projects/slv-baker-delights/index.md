## About

Started as an ambitious public-facing ordering app — think Blinkit, but for our family bakery. The plan was for customers to browse, order, and get deliveries directly through the app.

Somewhere along the way the scope shifted. The public-facing side was scaled back, and the app evolved into something more practical: an internal order management tool that the shop actually uses day-to-day to track and manage orders.

It started ambitious, became practical. And it still does its job every single day.
