## About

The first real website I ever built — made for our family bakery, SLV Bengaluru Bakery (since 2014).

This was my first experience building something for actual users with real needs. Not a tutorial project, not a portfolio piece — something my family actually used. That made it feel different. I learned more from this than from anything else I'd done before.

It taught me how to think about users first, how to handle real-world edge cases, and how much the gap is between "it works on my machine" and "it works for everyone".
